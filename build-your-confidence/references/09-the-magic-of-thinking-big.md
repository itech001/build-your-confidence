# The Magic of Thinking Big

**Author:** David J. Schwartz

## Key Quotes

1. "Believe you can succeed and you will."
2. "The size of your success is determined by the size of your belief."
3. "Think little goals and expect little achievements. Think big goals and win big success."
4. "When you believe something is impossible, your mind goes to work for you to prove why it can't be done."
5. "Believe it can be done. When you believe something can be done, really believe, your mind will find the ways to do it."
6. "The thinking that guides your intelligence is much more important than how much intelligence you have."
7. "Confidence is a tool you can use in your everyday life to do all kinds of things, to build confidence in others, too."
8. "The person who is worthy of being a leader is the one who can make decisions quickly and firmly."
9. "Your mind is a thought-producing machine. It produces thoughts all day long."
10. "The biggest mistake of all is to do nothing because you think you can only do a little."

## Practical Advice

- Think big in all your endeavors
- Don't sell yourself short
- Set ambitious goals
- Believe in your ability to succeed
- Take action despite fear or doubt
