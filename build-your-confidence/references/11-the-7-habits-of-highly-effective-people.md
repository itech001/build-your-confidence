# The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People

**Author:** Stephen R. Covey

## Key Quotes

1. "The key is not to prioritize what's on your schedule, but to schedule your priorities."
2. "Most people do not listen with the intent to understand; they listen with the intent to reply."
3. "Strength lies in differences, not in similarities."
4. "The main thing is to keep the main thing the main thing."
5. "Live out of your imagination, not your history."
6. "Trust is the glue of life. It's the foundational principle that holds all relationships."
7. "Sow a thought, reap an action; sow an action, reap a habit; sow a habit, reap a character; sow a character, reap a destiny."
8. "You have to decide what your highest priorities are and have the courage—pleasantly, smilingly, nonapologetically—to say no to other things."
9. "The way we see the problem is the problem."
10. "Be proactive, not reactive."

## The 7 Habits

1. **Be Proactive** - Take responsibility for your life
2. **Begin with the End in Mind** - Define your mission and goals
3. **Put First Things First** - Prioritize and execute
4. **Think Win-Win** - Seek mutual benefit
5. **Seek First to Understand, Then to Be Understood** - Listen before you speak
6. **Synergize** - Combine strengths through teamwork
7. **Sharpen the Saw** - Renew yourself regularly

## Practical Advice

- Start with the end in mind
- Put first things first
- Think win-win in all interactions
- Listen to understand, not to reply
- Renew yourself physically, mentally, socially, and spiritually
