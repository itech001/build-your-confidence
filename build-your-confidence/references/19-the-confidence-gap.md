# The Confidence Gap

**Author:** Russ Harris

## Key Quotes

1. "The confidence gap is the gap between where you are now and where you want to be."
2. "Confidence is not a feeling. It's a skill that can be developed."
3. "The more you try to feel confident, the more anxious you become."
4. "True confidence is not about eliminating fear; it's about learning to act despite fear."
5. "When you stop fighting your feelings, you free up energy to pursue your goals."
6. "The goal is not to feel confident all the time, but to act in line with your values."
7. "Confidence comes from taking action, not from waiting until you feel ready."
8. "The more you practice acting despite fear, the more confident you become."
9. "Your mind is not your friend. It's a tool that can help or hinder you."
10. "The secret to confidence is to focus on what you can control."

## Practical Advice

- Accept your feelings without trying to change them
- Focus on your values, not your feelings
- Take small, consistent actions
- Practice mindfulness
- Develop psychological flexibility
