# The Confidence Code

**Authors:** Katty Kay & Claire Shipman

## Key Quotes

1. "Confidence is the necessary spark before everything follows."
2. "Confidence is not about being perfect. It's about being willing to try."
3. "The confidence gap is not about ability—it's about the willingness to act."
4. "Women often wait until they're 100% qualified before applying for a job. Men apply when they're 60% qualified."
5. "Confidence comes from action, not from thinking about action."
6. "The more you practice confidence, the more natural it becomes."
7. "Failure is not the opposite of confidence; it's a stepping stone to confidence."
8. "Confidence is not about knowing you'll succeed; it's about knowing you'll be okay if you don't."
9. "The brain is wired to learn from experience. Every small success builds confidence."
10. "Confidence is a choice, not a trait. You can choose to act confidently."

## Practical Advice

- Start with small, achievable goals to build momentum
- Celebrate your successes, no matter how small
- Practice self-compassion when things don't go as planned
- Take action even when you don't feel ready
- Surround yourself with supportive people
