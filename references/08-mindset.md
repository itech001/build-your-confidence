# Mindset: The New Psychology of Success

**Author:** Carol S. Dweck

## Key Quotes

1. "Becoming is better than being."
2. "The view you adopt for yourself profoundly affects the way you lead your life."
3. "In a growth mindset, challenges are exciting rather than threatening."
4. "The passion for stretching yourself and sticking to it, even when it's not going well, is the hallmark of the growth mindset."
5. "No matter what your ability is, effort is what ignites that ability and turns it into accomplishment."
6. "In a growth mindset, failure can be a painful experience, but it doesn't define you."
7. "The growth mindset is based on the belief that your basic qualities are things you can cultivate through effort."
8. "When people believe their basic qualities can be developed, failures may still hurt, but they don't define you."
9. "The fixed mindset creates an urgency to prove yourself over and over."
10. "In a growth mindset, you don't always need to feel confident to take on challenges."

## Practical Advice

- Embrace challenges as opportunities to grow
- View effort as the path to mastery
- Learn from criticism and feedback
- Find inspiration in others' success
- Cultivate a love of learning
