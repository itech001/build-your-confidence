# Think and Grow Rich

**Author:** Napoleon Hill

## Key Quotes

1. "Whatever the mind can conceive and believe, it can achieve."
2. "The starting point of all achievement is desire."
3. "You are the master of your destiny. You can influence, direct, and control your own environment."
4. "The way to develop self-confidence is to do the thing you fear."
5. "Set your mind on a definite goal and observe how quickly the world stands aside to let you pass."
6. "Success comes to those who become success conscious."
7. "Every adversity brings with it the seed of an equivalent advantage."
8. "The mind is everything. What you think, you become."
9. "There are no limitations to the mind except those we acknowledge."
10. "Fear is nothing more than a state of mind."

## Practical Advice

- Define your definite purpose
- Develop a burning desire
- Have faith in your ability
- Use auto-suggestion to build confidence
- Take persistent action toward your goals
