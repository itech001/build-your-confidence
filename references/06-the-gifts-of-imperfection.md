# The Gifts of Imperfection

**Author:** Brené Brown

## Key Quotes

1. "Owning our story and loving ourselves through that process is the bravest thing that we'll ever do."
2. "Imperfections are not inadequacies; they are reminders that we're all in this together."
3. "You are imperfect, you are wired for struggle, but you are worthy of love and belonging."
4. "What makes you vulnerable makes you beautiful."
5. "Vulnerability is the birthplace of joy, creativity, belonging, and love."
6. "Authenticity is the daily practice of letting go of who we think we're supposed to be."
7. "The thing that is really hard, and really amazing, is giving up on being perfect and beginning the work of becoming yourself."
8. "You either walk inside your story and own it, or you stand outside your story and hustle for your worthiness."
9. "Belonging is the innate human desire to be part of something larger than us."
10. "Wholehearted living is about engaging in our lives from a place of worthiness."

## Practical Advice

- Practice self-compassion
- Embrace your imperfections
- Cultivate gratitude and joy
- Let go of who you think you should be
- Practice authenticity daily
