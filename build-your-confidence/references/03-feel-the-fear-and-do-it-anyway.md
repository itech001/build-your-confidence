# Feel the Fear and Do It Anyway

**Author:** Susan Jeffers

## Key Quotes

1. "The fear will never go away as long as I continue to grow."
2. "The only way to get rid of the fear of doing something is to go out and do it."
3. "Every time you encounter something that forces you to 'handle it,' your self-esteem is raised considerably."
4. "Fear is a natural reaction to moving closer to the truth."
5. "The worst thing that can happen is that you'll learn something."
6. "You can't get rid of the fear, but you can learn to move through it."
7. "The more you push through your fears, the more confident you become."
8. "Fear is not the problem. The problem is that we think we shouldn't have fear."
9. "When you push through fear, you feel exhilarated."
10. "The only way to build confidence is to take action."

## Practical Advice

- Start with small fears and work your way up
- Reframe fear as excitement
- Focus on what you can control
- Create a "fear list" and tackle one item at a time
- Celebrate every time you push through fear
