# You Are a Badass

**Author:** Jen Sincero

## Key Quotes

1. "You are a badass. You are. You really, really are."
2. "The only thing standing between you and your goal is the story you keep telling yourself."
3. "What you think about yourself is what the universe thinks about you."
4. "You are the only one who can limit your greatness."
5. "The more you love yourself, the less nonsense you'll tolerate."
6. "Fear is just excitement in disguise."
7. "You don't have to get it perfect, you just have to get it going."
8. "The universe is conspiring in your favor, even when it doesn't feel like it."
9. "Your life is your party. You get to choose who you invite."
10. "The only way to fail is to not try at all."

## Practical Advice

- Stop comparing yourself to others
- Embrace your uniqueness
- Take risks and embrace failure
- Practice gratitude daily
- Surround yourself with people who lift you up
