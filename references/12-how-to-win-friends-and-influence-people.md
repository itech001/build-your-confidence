# How to Win Friends and Influence People

**Author:** Dale Carnegie

## Key Quotes

1. "You can make more friends in two months by becoming interested in other people than you can in two years by trying to get other people interested in you."
2. "The only way to get the best of an argument is to avoid it."
3. "Any fool can criticize, condemn, and complain—and most fools do."
4. "A person's name is to that person the sweetest and most important sound in any language."
5. "Talk to someone about themselves and they will listen for hours."
6. "The royal road to a person's heart is to talk about the things he or she treasures most."
7. "If you want to be interesting, be interested."
8. "The successful leader is the one who can get people to do what they don't want to do and like it."
9. "Actions speak louder than words, and a smile says, 'I like you. You make me happy. I am glad to see you.'"
10. "You can't win an argument. You can't, because if you lose it, you lose it; and if you win it, you lose it."

## Practical Advice

- Become genuinely interested in other people
- Smile and be friendly
- Remember people's names
- Be a good listener
- Talk in terms of the other person's interests
- Make the other person feel important
