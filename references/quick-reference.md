## Quick Exercises

### 2-Minute Power Pose
From **Presence** by Amy Cuddy:
1. Stand in a confident posture
2. Take up space with your body
3. Breathe deeply and slowly
4. Visualize yourself succeeding
5. Trust in your abilities

### Self-Compassion Break
From **Self-Compassion** by Kristin Neff:
1. Acknowledge the suffering: "This is a moment of suffering."
2. Remember common humanity: "Suffering is a part of life."
3. Offer self-kindness: "May I be kind to myself in this moment."

### Values Clarification
From **The 7 Habits** by Stephen R. Covey:
1. Write down your top 5 values
2. For each value, write why it's important to you
3. Rate how well you're living each value (1-10)
4. Identify one action to better align with each value
