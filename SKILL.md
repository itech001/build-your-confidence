# Build Your Confidence / 建立自信

This skill provides guidance and wisdom from 40 classic self-confidence books (20 English + 20 Chinese) to help users build confidence, overcome self-doubt, and develop a strong sense of self-worth.

本技能提供来自40本经典自信书籍（20本英文+20本中文）的智慧和指导，帮助用户建立自信、克服自我怀疑、培养强烈的自我价值感。

## When to Use This Skill / 何时使用

Use this skill when the user:
- Expresses feelings of self-doubt or low self-esteem
- Needs encouragement or motivation
- Wants to build confidence in specific areas (work, relationships, public speaking, etc.)
- Asks for advice on overcoming fear or anxiety
- Seeks wisdom from self-help and personal development books
- Mentions: 自信, confidence, self-esteem, 自我怀疑, 自卑, 勇气, 内心力量, 自我提升, 自我成长

当用户出现以下情况时使用此技能：
- 表达自我怀疑或低自尊的感受
- 需要鼓励或动力
- 想要在特定领域建立自信（工作、人际关系、公开演讲等）
- 寻求克服恐惧或焦虑的建议
- 寻找自助和个人发展书籍的智慧

## How to Use This Skill / 使用方法

### Step 1: Understand the User's Need / 第一步：理解用户需求

Identify what aspect of confidence the user is struggling with:
- General self-confidence
- Social confidence
- Professional confidence
- Public speaking confidence
- Decision-making confidence
- Body image and self-acceptance

识别用户在哪个方面缺乏自信：
- 一般自信心
- 社交自信
- 职业自信
- 公开演讲自信
- 决策自信
- 身体形象和自我接纳

### Step 2: Choose Relevant Books / 第二步：选择相关书籍

Select quotes and advice from the most relevant books. Use Chinese books for Chinese users, English books for English users, or mix both for bilingual users.

**English Books:** `references/` directory (01-20)
**Chinese Books:** `references/chinese/` directory (01-20)

为用户选择最相关的书籍引用。对中文用户使用中文书籍，对英文用户使用英文书籍，或为双语用户混合使用。

### Step 3: Provide Wisdom / 第三步：提供智慧

1. **Start with empathy** - Acknowledge the user's feelings / 以共情开始 - 承认用户的感受
2. **Share relevant quotes** - Provide 1-3 powerful quotes / 分享相关引用 - 提供1-3条有力的引言
3. **Offer practical advice** - Suggest actionable steps / 提供实用建议 - 建议可执行的步骤
4. **Encourage reflection** - Ask thoughtful questions / 鼓励反思 - 提出深思的问题

### Step 4: Follow Up / 第四步：跟进

- Check if the user wants more specific advice
- Suggest related books from the references
- Offer to help with specific confidence-building exercises

- 检查用户是否需要更具体的建议
- 推荐相关的书籍
- 帮助进行具体的自信建立练习

## Example Usage / 使用示例

### English Example

**User:** "I'm feeling really insecure about my upcoming presentation at work."

**Response:**
1. Acknowledge the feeling: "It's completely normal to feel nervous about presentations. Many successful people experience this too."
2. Share relevant wisdom: "In 'The Confidence Code,' Katty Kay writes: 'Confidence is the necessary spark before everything follows.' And Amy Cuddy in 'Presence' suggests that adopting a 'power pose' before your presentation can actually change how you feel."
3. Offer practical advice: "Try this: Before your presentation, find a private space and stand in a confident posture for 2 minutes. Take deep breaths and remind yourself of your expertise."
4. Encourage reflection: "What specific aspect of the presentation worries you most? We can work through that together."

### 中文示例

**用户：** "我对下周的工作演讲感到非常不安。"

**回复：**
1. 承认感受："对演讲感到紧张是完全正常的。很多成功的人都经历过这种感觉。"
2. 分享智慧："'被讨厌的勇气'中写道：'自由就是被别人讨厌。'而'自信的陷阱'则告诉我们：'自信不是一种感觉，而是一种行动。'"
3. 提供实用建议："试试这个：演讲前，找一个私密的空间，站成自信的姿势2分钟。深呼吸，提醒自己你的专业能力。"
4. 鼓励反思："演讲的哪个方面让你最担心？我们可以一起解决。"
