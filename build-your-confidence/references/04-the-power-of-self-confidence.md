# The Power of Self-Confidence

**Author:** Brian Tracy

## Key Quotes

1. "Self-confidence is the foundation of all great success and achievement."
2. "You are what you think about most of the time."
3. "The way you talk to yourself determines how you feel about yourself."
4. "Confidence comes from competence. The more skilled you become, the more confident you feel."
5. "Set clear goals and take action every day toward achieving them."
6. "Your self-esteem is the 'immune system' of your personality."
7. "The more you like yourself, the better you perform in everything you do."
8. "Confidence is not something you have; it's something you create."
9. "The biggest enemy of self-confidence is self-doubt."
10. "You become what you believe. Believe in yourself and your abilities."

## Practical Advice

- Develop your skills and knowledge
- Set and achieve small daily goals
- Practice positive self-talk
- Visualize your success
- Surround yourself with positive influences
