## How to Use

### Installation
```bash
cd build-your-confidence
./install.sh
```

### Triggering the Skill
The skill activates when users mention:
- 自信, confidence, self-esteem
- 自我怀疑, 自卑, 勇气
- 内心力量, 自我提升, 自我成长
- motivational, inspirational

### Example Usage
**User:** "I'm feeling really insecure about my upcoming presentation."

**Agent Response:**
1. Acknowledge the feeling with empathy
2. Share relevant quotes from "The Confidence Code" and "Presence"
3. Offer practical advice (power poses, preparation tips)
4. Encourage reflection on specific concerns
