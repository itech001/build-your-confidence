# Awaken the Giant Within

**Author:** Tony Robbins

## Key Quotes

1. "The only limit to your impact is your imagination and commitment."
2. "It's not what we do once in a while that shapes our lives, but what we do consistently."
3. "The path to success is to take massive, determined action."
4. "Your life changes the moment you make a new, congruent, and committed decision."
5. "It is in your moments of decision that your destiny is shaped."
6. "The quality of your life is the quality of your relationships."
7. "What we can or cannot do, what we consider possible or impossible, is rarely a function of our true capability."
8. "The only thing that's keeping you from getting what you want is the story you keep telling yourself."
9. "Success is doing what you want to do, when you want, where you want, with whom you want, as much as you want."
10. "The secret of success is learning how to use pain and pleasure instead of having pain and pleasure use you."

## Practical Advice

- Make clear, committed decisions
- Take massive action toward your goals
- Master your emotions
- Create empowering beliefs
- Build positive habits
