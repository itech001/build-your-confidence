# Presence: Bringing Your Boldest Self to Your Biggest Challenges

**Author:** Amy Cuddy

## Key Quotes

1. "Presence is the state of being attuned to and able to comfortably express our true thoughts, feelings, values, and potential."
2. "Our bodies change our minds, and our minds change our behavior, and our behavior changes our outcomes."
3. "When we feel powerful, we feel confident. When we feel confident, we are present."
4. "The way you carry yourself matters. It's not just about how others see you—it's about how you see yourself."
5. "Fake it till you become it. Don't fake it till you make it."
6. "Your body language shapes who you are."
7. "When you pretend to be powerful, you start to feel powerful."
8. "Presence is not about being perfect. It's about being present."
9. "The most powerful thing you can do is be yourself."
10. "You have more power than you think."

## The Power Pose

- Stand in a confident posture for 2 minutes
- Take up space with your body
- Breathe deeply and slowly
- Visualize yourself succeeding
- Trust in your abilities

## Practical Advice

- Practice power poses before important events
- Focus on your breathing
- Be present in the moment
- Trust your preparation
- Embrace your authentic self
