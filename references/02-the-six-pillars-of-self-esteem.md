# The Six Pillars of Self-Esteem

**Author:** Nathaniel Branden

## Key Quotes

1. "Self-esteem is the reputation we acquire with ourselves."
2. "The first step toward change is awareness. The second step is acceptance."
3. "No one is coming to save you. Your life is 100% your responsibility."
4. "Self-esteem is not a luxury; it is a profound spiritual need."
5. "To live consciously means to seek to be aware of everything that bears on our actions, purposes, values, and goals."
6. "The more you respect yourself, the more you can respect others."
7. "Self-esteem is not about being better than others; it's about being true to yourself."
8. "When we deny our feelings, we lose touch with who we really are."
9. "The greatest power we have is the power to choose our response to any situation."
10. "Integrity is the integration of ideals, convictions, standards, beliefs—and behavior."

## The Six Pillars

1. **Living Consciously** - Being present and aware
2. **Self-Acceptance** - Embracing all aspects of yourself
3. **Self-Responsibility** - Taking ownership of your life
4. **Self-Assertiveness** - Honoring your needs and wants
5. **Living Purposefully** - Setting and pursuing meaningful goals
6. **Personal Integrity** - Aligning actions with values

## Practical Advice

- Practice daily self-reflection
- Identify and challenge negative self-talk
- Set boundaries that honor your values
- Take responsibility for your choices
- Align your actions with your beliefs
